Workout Plans

Lean Workout Plan:

Monday

Full Body Strength Training

  • Warm-up: 10 minutes of light cardio, dynamic stretches
  • Strength Training:
    • Squats: 3 sets x 12 reps
    • Push-ups: 3 sets x 15 reps
    • Bent-over Rows: 3 sets x 12 reps
    • Lunges: 3 sets x 10 reps per leg
    • Plank: 3 sets x 30 seconds
  • Cardio Finisher: HIIT - 15 minutes

Tuesday

Cardio and Core

  • Cardio: 30 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio (running, cycling, elliptical, etc.)
  • Core Workout:
    • Bicycle Crunches: 3 sets x 20 reps
    • Russian Twists: 3 sets x 15 reps per side
    • Plank with Leg Raises: 3 sets x 15 reps per leg
    • Mountain Climbers: 3 sets x 20 reps

Wednesday

Active Recovery or Rest: Light activities like walking, yoga, or stretching to aid recovery.

Thursday

Upper Body Strength Training

  • Warm-up: 10 minutes of light cardio, dynamic stretches
  • Strength Training (Upper Body):
    • Bench Press: 3 sets x 12 reps
    • Pull-ups or Lat Pulldowns: 3 sets x 10 reps
    • Bent-over Rows: 3 sets x 12 reps
    • Overhead Press: 3 sets x 12 reps
    • Dumbbell Rows: 3 sets x 12 reps per arm
    • Plank: 3 sets x 30 seconds
  • Cardio Finisher: Jump Rope: 15 minutes

Friday

Interval Cardio HIIT Cardio: 20 minutes (alternating between high-intensity and low-intensity intervals)

Saturday

Lower Body and Core

  • Warm-up: 10 minutes of light cardio, dynamic stretches
  • Strength Training (Lower Body):
    • Deadlifts: 3 sets x 10 reps
    • Leg Press: 3 sets x 12 reps
    • Leg Curls: 3 sets x 15 reps
    • Calf Raises: 3 sets x 20 reps
  • Core Workout:
    • Plank Variations: 3 sets x 30 seconds each
    • Leg Raises: 3 sets x 15 reps
    • Side Planks: 3 sets x 20 seconds each side

Sunday

Rest or Active Recovery: Rest day or light activities for recovery.
